The Obligation

CBAM is live. Are you
above 50 tonnes?

From January 2026, EU importers above 50 tonnes/year of CBAM-covered goods must register as Authorized Declarants and comply with three mandatory tasks.

1

Register as Authorized CBAM Declarant

Registration is mandatory. Without it, you cannot file your declaration and face potential customs suspension.

2

Calculate embedded carbon per shipment

Using EU Commission methodology (Implementing Regulation 2023/1603). Manual spreadsheets are complex and error-prone.

3

File annual CBAM declaration by Sep 30, 2027

Submit to the EU CBAM Registry in Annex IV format. Incorrect declarations carry financial penalties.

Current options: DIY with EU Commission Excel (complex, no guided UI) — or enterprise tools starting at €10,000+/year. Nothing in between. Until now.
