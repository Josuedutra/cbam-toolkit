What is CBAM?

The EU Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism turns import data into a compliance workflow.

CBAM requires EU importers in carbon-intensive sectors to track embedded emissions, gather supplier evidence, and submit quarterly transitional reports. The hard part is not knowing the rule exists; it is keeping supplier data, product codes, emissions factors, and report deadlines aligned.
